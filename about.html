    Founded by Joan in 2024, Fluent Waves is an innovative English teaching school designed to harness Joan’s extensive experience teaching thousands of students from diverse backgrounds around the globe. With a vision to enhance the learning experience, Joan created Fluent Waves to combine her unique teaching style with a comprehensive library of tailored resources.
